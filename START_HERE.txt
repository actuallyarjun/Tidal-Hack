================================================================
            CAMERA CALIBRATION (OPTIONAL)
================================================================

Distances seem wrong? Calibrate in 2 minutes!

1. Stand exactly 2 meters from camera

2. App detects you, shows distance (e.g., "2.8m")

3. Calculate: 2.0 / 2.8 = 0.71

4. Copy env.example to .env

5. Add: CALIBRATION_FACTOR=0.71

6. Restart RUN_THIS.bat

7. Now distances are accurate!

See CALIBRATION_GUIDE.md for full instructions.
